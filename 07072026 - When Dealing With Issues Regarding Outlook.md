It is very important to understand that the UI for Outlook and Outlook Classic look very different.

So when assisting a customer about an issue with Outlook, make sure to clarify what version of Outlook they are using.

Update 07/08/2026:

- Turns out the issue as to why Maira couldn't find emails from the sender was because she had spelled their name incorrectly (Antony vs Anthony)
- Regardless, from connecting to her computer via Remote Desktop, she had her Outlook set to "Legacy mode". This goes to show that there are many variables when solving what is considered a fairly simple ticket. It is important to ask questions in order to eliminate these possible variables.
