**After Approval is Given:**

Go to [Ingram portal](https://usa.ingrammicro.com/cep/app/my/dashboard):
1. On the left-side menu, click on **My business** -> **Quotes
2. Find the quote that matches the quote ID received from Fortinet-Licensing@ingrammicro.com
3. Under **Quote ID** you will find a drop down menu named **End Customer**
4. **PO number** will be the Autotask ticket number & **Customer PO number** will be the company's name
	- IIRC: There will be a section for **phone number**, **zip code**, and maybe **address**?
		- Put down Skyward IT company information for those fields
		![[Pasted image 20260713163236.png]]
5. After placing the order, go to the ticket on Autotask, and click on **Charges & Expenses**
	- **Billable Amount** will be the MSRP amount, and **Extended Cost** will be the amount listed on Ingram
	- Normally the description will follow this format: FortiGate-{**Model Number**} 1-Year Renewal
	- Material Code will be **Licenses and Subscriptions**
		![[Pasted image 20260713164829.png]]
6. After hitting **Save and Close**, go to Outlook and send an email to purchasing@skywardit.com
	- In the body
		- {**Company Name**} FortiGate Renewal
		- {**Autotask Ticket Number**}
		![[Pasted image 20260713164618.png]]
7. **And you are done!**
