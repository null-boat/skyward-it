**First Email:** 
Hi {Name of Customer},

I am reaching out to you regarding your device needing to be upgraded from Windows 10 to Windows 11. The process will take approximately 1 ½ hours to 2 hours to complete. During the time of installation, you will still be able to work on your device as normal; however, I will be monitoring the upgrade to ensure that it installs properly. 

Please let me know when your best availability would be for me to perform this upgrade.

---

**When Client Responds**:
Hi {Customer Name},

Great! I'll be connecting to your desktop shortly.

Thank you in advance.

Best regards, 

---

**When Notifying Client About Remote Desktop**
Hi {Customer Name},

Okay, sounds great. I'll be remoting onto your device shortly.

Thank you in advance,

---

- It is important to notify the client when you will be connecting to their computer.

- Sometimes if a client has not rebooted his computer for a while, the installer may take a lot longer than it should during Step 3 of the installation. So **MAKE SURE TO REBOOT ALWAYS BEFORE PROCEEDING WITH UPGRADE!!**
