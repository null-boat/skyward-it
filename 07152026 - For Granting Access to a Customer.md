You must always, ALWAYS, **ALWAYS** ask for approval to the manager or, in our case, the person-of-contact (POC).

Situations where approval is required is:
- Access to SharePoint sites
- Access to specific drives
- Creating SharePoint sites and folders

So far these are what have occurred during my time here, but when in doubt always ask for approval to save your own ass.

IT Glue will have the person of contact for the organization under **Quick Notes**.
